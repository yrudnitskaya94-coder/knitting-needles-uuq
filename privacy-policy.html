Privacy Policy

1. What We CollectWhen you visit our site, make a purchase, or contact us, we may collect:
  • Personal details like your name, email, shipping address, and phone number
  • Order information such as products purchased and payment details (processed securely by trusted providers)
  • Browsing data like your IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via cookies and analytics)

2. How We Use ItWe use your information to:
  • Process orders and deliver products
  • Respond to your messages or questions
  • Send updates or newsletters (only if you’ve opted in)
  • Improve our website and customer experience

3. Keeping Your Info SafeYour information is stored securely and accessed only when necessary. We do not store your credit card details — all payments are handled through secure, third-party services.

4. Sharing InformationWe only share your data with trusted service providers, such as:
  • Payment processors
  • Shipping companies
  • Website analytics and hosting services
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

5. CookiesWe use cookies to:
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic and usage
  • Improve our site performance
You can manage or block cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your ChoicesYou can:
  • Unsubscribe from emails anytime
  • Ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Disable cookies via your browser


7. Children’s PrivacyWe do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 13. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided information, please contact us.

8. Policy UpdatesWe may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, with the new date at the top.

9. Contact UsHave questions? We’re here to help.
